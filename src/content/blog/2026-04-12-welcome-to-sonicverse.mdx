We've been building in the open for a while now, and today we're ready to talk about it properly.

**Sonicverse** is a suite of open-source tools for independent media operators — radio stations, podcasters, and community broadcasters who want full control over their infrastructure without the cost of enterprise platforms.

## Why we're building this

Independent media has always punched above its weight on creativity. What it hasn't had is the engineering infrastructure to match. We want to close that gap.

<Callout title="Our commitment" eyebrow="Open source">
  Every project we ship is self-hostable and permissively licensed. No lock-in, no hidden tiers.
</Callout>

## What's in the stack

The first project out of the door is the **Audio Streaming Stack** — a fully self-hostable streaming setup designed for live audio delivery at the scale that independent broadcasters actually need.

## What comes next

We're working on more projects in the areas of:

<Steps title="Upcoming areas" eyebrow="Roadmap">
  1. Audience analytics — privacy-first, self-hosted listener data
  2. Monetisation tooling — subscriptions and donations without platform cuts
  3. Scheduling — open-source playout and automation for radio
</Steps>
