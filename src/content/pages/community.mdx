We keep contribution pathways clear and inclusive. Whether you write code, improve docs, create demos, or host community calls, you are helping expand access to creative technology.

## Contribution lanes

- **Core platform**: Runtime, APIs, and SDKs
- **Ecosystem**: Integrations, plugins, starter templates
- **Documentation**: Tutorials, examples, migration guides
- **Community**: Events, translation, moderation, support
