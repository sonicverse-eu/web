Sonicverse started from a simple frustration: too many creative audio workflows were trapped in closed systems. We believe open standards and open code are the best way to accelerate innovation.

## How we work

- Public roadmap and transparent decision logs
- Community-led RFC process for major features
- A contributor-first culture with mentorship tracks

## What success looks like

A world where creators and developers can plug into a shared sonic stack, remix ideas freely, and ship meaningful products without platform lock-in.
